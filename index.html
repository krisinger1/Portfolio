Tennis Buddy App

I created an app for my husband to notify him when there is good weather coming up for playing tennis. The app gets weather data from the internet and displays time periods where the conditions are suitable for outdoor playing. Once a day, the user receives a notification to remind them to look at the list of tennis times the app has found. Clicking the notification takes the user to a list of new tennis times. Clicking on an item in the list takes user to detail view where they can invite a friend to play by email or message, add the event to their calendar, or reject that time because they are unavailable. User can also go to a map view to choose a location on the map where they want to play tennis at that time. This can be a custom location or a Point of Interest.
